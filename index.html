🛡️ ShieldCall

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

🔒 No Data Collection

ShieldCall does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data — including blocked numbers, call logs, and app settings — remains entirely on your device.

We do not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, location, or contacts.

📱 Contacts Access

ShieldCall requests access to your contacts solely to identify trusted callers and prevent accidental blocking. This information is used only on your device and is never uploaded or shared.

📞 Permissions

The app uses the following permissions strictly for functionality:

  • READ_CALL_LOG — call screening
  • READ_PHONE_STATE — detect incoming calls
  • READ_PHONE_NUMBERS — spoofing protection
  • READ_CONTACTS — trusted caller identification
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS — blocked call alerts

🗓️ Data Retention

All data remains on your device and is never transmitted externally.

You can clear call logs at any time. Blocked numbers and settings remain until removed manually.

🛡️ Security

All data is stored locally using encrypted storage protected by Android's built-in security. ShieldCall does not use servers or accounts, meaning there is no remote access to your data.

📢 Advertising

ShieldCall uses third-party advertising services such as Google AdMob. These services may collect device identifiers and usage data to display relevant ads.

Learn more: https://policies.google.com/privacy

👶 Children's Privacy

ShieldCall is not intended for children under 13. No personal data is collected from any users.

📝 Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the app means you accept any updates.
