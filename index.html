🛡️ ShieldCall

Privacy Policy

🔒

No Data Collection

ShieldCall does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data — including blocked numbers, call logs, and app settings — remains entirely on your device.

ShieldCall does not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, location, or contacts for storage or transmission.

📱

Contacts Access

ShieldCall requests access to your contacts solely to identify trusted callers and prevent accidental blocking. This information is used only on your device and is never uploaded, shared, or stored externally.

📞

Call Log and Permissions

ShieldCall uses certain permissions strictly to provide its core functionality:

  • READ_CALL_LOG — to support call screening
  • READ_PHONE_STATE — to detect incoming calls
  • READ_PHONE_NUMBERS — to identify your number for spoofing protection
  • READ_CONTACTS — to recognize trusted callers
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS — to notify you of blocked calls

All call log data is stored locally in an encrypted database on your device.

🗓️

Data Retention

All data remains on your device and is never transmitted externally.

You have full control over your data:

  • Call logs can be cleared at any time from within the app
  • Blocked numbers and settings remain until you remove them manually
🛡️

Security

All app data is stored locally using encrypted storage protected by Android's built-in security systems.

ShieldCall does not use external servers or user accounts, meaning there is no remote access to your data.

📢

Advertising

ShieldCall uses third-party advertising services, including Google AdMob.

These services may collect device identifiers and limited usage data to provide relevant advertisements. This data collection is governed by Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

ShieldCall itself does not collect or share personal data with advertisers.

👶

Children's Privacy

ShieldCall is not directed at children under the age of 13.

Since no personal data is collected by the app, no data from children is knowingly collected or stored.

📝

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be reflected within the app with a revised effective date.

Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
