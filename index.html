ShieldCall

Privacy First

ShieldCall is designed to protect your privacy and give you full control over incoming calls.

🔒 No Data Collection

ShieldCall does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

All processing happens locally on your device.

📞 How It Works

ShieldCall uses Android's Call Screening technology to analyze incoming calls and block unwanted calls before they reach you.

Call data is processed in real-time and never leaves your device.

👤 Contacts Access

Used only to identify trusted callers and avoid blocking people you know.

Contact data stays on your device.

⚙️ Permissions

  • READ_CONTACTS – identify known callers
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS – show blocked call alerts

ShieldCall does not read your system call history.

📂 Local Storage

Only blocked calls are stored locally on your device.

Allowed calls are never recorded.

📢 Advertising

ShieldCall uses Google AdMob for ads.

Learn more: Google Privacy Policy
