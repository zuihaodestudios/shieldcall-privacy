🛡️ ShieldCall

Privacy Policy

🔒

No Data Collection

ShieldCall does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

All processing is performed locally on your device. Your phone numbers, contacts, and call information never leaves your device.

📞

How ShieldCall Works

ShieldCall uses Android's Call Screening technology to analyze incoming calls and block unwanted calls before they reach you.

This process happens entirely on your device and does not involve any external servers.

👤

Contacts Access

ShieldCall requests access to your contacts only to identify trusted callers and prevent accidental blocking.

This information is used locally on your device and is never uploaded, shared, or stored externally.

⚙️

Permissions Usage

ShieldCall requests only the permissions necessary to provide its core functionality:

  • READ_CONTACTS — used locally to identify known callers and avoid blocking contacts
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS — used to notify you when a call has been blocked

ShieldCall does not access or read your system call history.

📂

Local Data Storage

ShieldCall may store blocked call records and app settings locally on your device.

This data is never transmitted externally and can be cleared at any time within the app.

🛡️

Security

All data is stored locally using Android's built-in security protections.

Since ShieldCall does not use external servers or user accounts, your data is not accessible remotely.

📢

Advertising

ShieldCall uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect limited device information to provide relevant ads.

This data is handled by Google and is subject to their privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

ShieldCall does not collect or share personal data with advertisers.

👶

Children's Privacy

ShieldCall is not directed toward children under the age of 13.

The app does not knowingly collect personal data from anyone, including children.

📝

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.

Any updates will be reflected on this page with a revised effective date.
